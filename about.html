About Yamato Japanese Steakhouse

Welcome to Yamato Japanese Steakhouse, where we bring the authentic flavors of Japan to Dunn, NC. Since opening our doors, we have been dedicated to providing an exceptional dining experience with a focus on quality ingredients, expert preparation, and friendly service.

At Yamato, we specialize in a wide range of traditional Japanese dishes, from sizzling hibachi and teppanyaki to expertly crafted sushi rolls. Each dish is made with care, combining fresh, high-quality ingredients to deliver bold flavors that will transport your taste buds straight to Japan.

Whether you choose to dine in and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of our restaurant or prefer the convenience of takeout and delivery, we promise a meal that is both satisfying and memorable. Our skilled chefs are known for their interactive hibachi cooking, providing a fun, entertaining experience for guests of all ages.
